# Contribution Guidelines

We welcome contributions to enhance our Airplane and Reminder Service!

## Reminder Service Ideas

Here are some ideas for a nice reminder service:

*   **Personalized Reminders**: Allow users to set reminders for various events (e.g., "flight check-in", "take off time", "landing time", "medication", "meetings").
*   **Location-Based Reminders**: Trigger reminders when a user enters or leaves a specific location (e.g., "remind me to check my luggage when I arrive at the airport").
*   **Time-Zone Aware Reminders**: Automatically adjust reminder times based on the user's current time zone, especially useful for travelers.
*   **Flight Status Integration**: Integrate with flight tracking APIs to send reminders about flight delays, gate changes, or boarding times automatically.
*   **Recurring Reminders**: Support daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly recurring reminders.
*   **Customizable Notifications**: Allow users to choose notification methods (e.g., email, SMS, push notifications) and customize alert sounds.
*   **Snooze/Dismiss Options**: Provide easy ways to snooze a reminder for a short period or dismiss it permanently.
*   **Pre-defined Reminder Templates**: Offer templates for common travel-related reminders.
*   **Integration with Calendars**: Sync reminders with popular calendar applications.
